About Me

Who I Am

I am Anmol Singhal, also known by the alias Rexden. I build systems, open-source tools, and educational projects intended to be usable and forkable by learners everywhere. I live in Uttar Pradesh, India. I favor simplicity and reliability — that’s why I publish plain, replicable templates and guides (like ProLava).

My Goal & Mission

My mission is to remove financial barriers to learning — provide free, production-ready tools and teaching resources so anyone can learn to build and ship projects without expensive courses. I document everything and keep the stack lean so low-end devices can run and understand projects easily.

My Entrepreneur Experience

I’ve built, launched, and maintained open-source projects for community learning and production use. I focus on clear folder structure, deployment scripts, and practical guides so others can deploy quickly on VPS or Docker with minimal friction.

Skills & Behaviour

Daily stack: JavaScript, Python, Node, Next.js, FastAPI, PostgreSQL. I prefer maintainable code, detailed docs, and helpful community support. I iterate fast but keep releases stable.

Interests & Future

I enjoy worldbuilding, fiction system design, and combining narrative with tooling. Future plans: more tutorials, stable templates, and community workshops.
