# ProSite Code of Conduct

This project follows a strict code of conduct to maintain a respectful, professional, and safe environment for everyone.

---

## Our Values

- Respect for all contributors
- Open collaboration
- Professional behavior
- Honest communication
- No discrimination or harassment

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment of any kind
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Threats or personal attacks
- Spamming or malicious activity
- Disruptive or abusive conduct

---

## Enforcement

The project maintainer has the right to remove any contributor or content that violates these rules without prior warning.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior, report it immediately through official contact channels.

---

This code of conduct applies to all spaces related to the ProSite project.
